End Of Season Competition

To celebrate the end of a great season of training the cadets are holding an end of season competition designed to be a great chance to have one final bit of fun before beach training packs up for the winter months. A massive THANK YOU to all the instrouctors who made this season possible! We're all so gratefull for the time you give us. Events will include:

⚪ Board Relay⚪ Swim Relay ⚪ Beach Flags ⚪ Beach Relay ⚪ All on Board Race ⚪ Tube Relay ⚪
